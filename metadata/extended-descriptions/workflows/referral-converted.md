### What this template solves

- Closes the loop for referrers when their invitee takes meaningful action beyond signup.
- Motivates continued referral activity by showing real progress.
- Distinguishes between "accepted invite" and "became an active user."
- Uses in-app-first delivery to keep referrers engaged without email overload.

---

### When to use this template

Use this template when:

- Your referral program rewards actions beyond just accepting an invite.
- Conversion might happen days or weeks after the initial referral acceptance.
- You want to notify referrers at the moment that matters for reward eligibility.
- You already have a referral-accepted workflow and need the next funnel stage.

---

### How it works (step-by-step)

1. **Trigger.** Referred user completes a conversion action (first project, first purchase, etc.).
2. **In-app notification.** Referrer sees a celebration message with referral progress.
3. **Wait 8 hours.** Allow time for the in-app message to be seen naturally.
4. **Email if unseen.** Send a follow-up email only if the in-app notification wasn't read.

---

### Best practices

- **Define conversion clearly.** Document what actions count — signup alone usually isn't enough.
- **Name the action.** "{{ name }} completed their first project" is more motivating than "Referral converted."
- **Show reward progress.** If rewards are pending, explain what's left to earn the full payout.
- **Encourage more referrals.** Gently suggest sending another invite while enthusiasm is high.

---

### Common mistakes to avoid

- **Notifying too early.** Wait for genuine activation, not just account creation.
- **Duplicate with referral-accepted.** Accepted and converted are different moments — use both workflows.
- **Ignoring fraud.** Verify conversion actions are legitimate before notifying and issuing rewards.

---

### FAQ

**What's a good conversion action?**  
 First meaningful product use: creating a project, sending a notification, completing onboarding, or making a purchase.

**Should I notify the referred user too?**  
 Consider it. A "Your friend will earn a reward thanks to you" message reinforces the social connection.

**How is this different from referral-accepted?**  
 Accepted fires when the invite is accepted. Converted fires when the invitee completes a valuable action.
