### What this template solves

- Delivers the payoff moment that motivates future referral activity.
- Handles reward hold periods before notifying users their credit is available.
- Closes the full referral loop from invite to payout with a clear celebration.
- Combines in-app and email to ensure referrers never miss their reward.

---

### When to use this template

Use this template when:

- Your referral program offers credits, cash, or discounts as rewards.
- Rewards have a hold period for fraud prevention or refund windows.
- You want a distinct notification for "reward earned" vs. "referral converted."
- Referrers need a clear CTA to claim or view their reward balance.

---

### How it works (step-by-step)

1. **Trigger.** Referral conversion is verified and reward eligibility is confirmed.
2. **Hold period.** Wait 7 days to allow for refunds, chargebacks, or fraud review.
3. **In-app notification.** Tell the referrer their reward is ready with a claim link.
4. **Email confirmation.** Send an email with reward amount, type, and claim button.

---

### Best practices

- **State the exact reward.** "$10 credit" is clearer than "You earned a reward."
- **Explain the hold period.** Tell users upfront why rewards take time to process.
- **Make claiming easy.** One-click claim button that applies credit automatically.
- **Encourage repeat referrals.** Show total earned and how close they are to the next tier.

---

### Common mistakes to avoid

- **Surprise hold periods.** Communicate the delay when the referral converts, not just when the reward arrives.
- **Vague reward types.** Specify whether it's credit, cash, discount, or extended trial.
- **Not handling revocations.** Cancel the workflow if the referred user's action is reversed during the hold.

---

### FAQ

**How long should the hold period be?**  
 7–14 days is standard for SaaS. Match your refund window or billing cycle.

**Should I auto-apply rewards or require claiming?**  
 Auto-apply reduces friction. Require claiming only if you need explicit user acknowledgment.

**What if the reward amount varies?**  
 Pass the amount and type in the trigger payload. The templates support dynamic values.
