### What this template solves

- Converts free trial users to paid customers before access expires.
- Gives users multiple touchpoints to upgrade without feeling ambushed.
- Creates urgency with a timed sequence at 7, 3, and 1 days remaining.
- Reduces involuntary churn from users who simply forgot their trial was ending.

---

### When to use this template

Use this template when:

- Your product offers a time-limited free trial.
- Trial-to-paid conversion is a key growth metric.
- Users need advance notice to get budget approval or evaluate the product.
- You want a proven email sequence without building it from scratch.

---

### How it works (step-by-step)

1. **Trigger.** Your system triggers the workflow when 7 days remain on the trial.
2. **7-day email.** First reminder with trial end date and upgrade link.
3. **Wait 4 days, then 3-day email.** Increased urgency as the deadline approaches.
4. **Wait 2 days, then last-day email.** Final reminder on the last day of the trial.
5. **Cancel on upgrade.** Cancel the workflow if the user converts to a paid plan.

---

### Best practices

- **Lead with value.** Remind users what they'll lose, not just that the trial ends.
- **Include the end date.** Specific dates create more urgency than "your trial is ending soon."
- **Make upgrading easy.** One-click upgrade link with pre-selected plan when possible.
- **Personalize by usage.** Reference what the user built during their trial.

---

### Common mistakes to avoid

- **Starting too late.** Seven days gives users time to decide; a single last-day email converts poorly.
- **Continuing after upgrade.** Cancel immediately when the user converts — nothing erodes trust faster.
- **Aggressive tone on day 7.** Save urgency for the final emails; the first should be informational.

---

### FAQ

**When exactly should I trigger this workflow?**  
 Trigger once when exactly 7 days remain. The internal delays handle the 3-day and last-day emails.

**What if trials are different lengths?**  
 Adjust the trigger timing in your app. The workflow sequence works for any trial length if triggered at 7 days out.

**Should I add in-app notifications too?**  
 Yes, for high-value trials. Pair this email sequence with in-app banners on the last 3 days.
